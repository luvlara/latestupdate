About NJ Valdez Colleges Foundation Inc

NJ Valdez Colleges Foundation Inc has been a cornerstone of quality education, nurturing generations of dedicated professionals, servant leaders, and lifelong learners committed to nation-building and community service.

🎯

Our Mission

NJ Valdez Colleges Foundation mission is to achieve and maintain economic development and social progress resulting to the growth of students, professionals and individuals as its preserves and motes desirable cultural moral , and spiritual valves in globally competitive world.

👁️

Our Vision

NJ Valdez Colleges Foundation is envisioned to acquire vital educational foundation for nurturing and enhancing the quality of life of every individual as their full potentials are realized to greatest extend. It shall in calculate promotion of developing professionals who are strengthened by knowledge, excellence and values education.
